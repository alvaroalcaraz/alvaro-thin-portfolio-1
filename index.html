¡Hola illo!

I'm Álvaro, a UX/UI Designer from Cádiz, based in Barcelona. Food, sport and travel lover.

Designing products is my biggest passion, products that make people’s lives more enjoyable.

Some history

It was 2009 and I was living in Cádiz, where I was born and raise, since always and my mum decided to move to Barcelona. At that moment my life was broken and I was lost. I didn't know how important how that will change my life completely.

10 years after I was already being living not only in Barcelona but London, United Kindom, Strasbourg, France and Charleroi, Belgium and I decided to study UX/UI Design, I already had a lot of experience in Tourism and Customer service and that help me empathize more with the users and make me work harder to make their lives easier.
